bloom.api

an api to search and compare physicians

Current

BloomAPI is updated weekly with the lastest details from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS). The data is pulled regularly from the National Provider Identifer (NPI) data dissemnation. Programmatically available health information ensures you're up to date.

Free

Free, as in speech. In addition to being hosted for free, non-commercial use, BloomAPI's full source code is available online with a MIT license so you can run your own to meet your SLA requirements. Deploy it yourself for higher performance or high availability.

Performant

Built with high performance in mind, we strive to maintain 200ms or less response time on any possible NPI search. We actively monitor performance and optimize as is needed to ensure support for applications that need NPI data realtime.

Accessible

Need to leverage the full NPI dataset? Run your own BloomAPI deployment for direct database access. BloomAPI will ensure your NPI data is always fresh without needing to regularly maintain your deployment.
